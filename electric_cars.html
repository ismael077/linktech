Electric Cars
Before starting...
Electric cars are vehicles powered by one or more electric motors that use the energy stored in the battery. They are silent and do not pollute, they only need a cable and a plug to charge their battery. With an electric vehicle you can move around the city without fear of possible fines for parking or circulation in episodes of pollution and in periods with restrictions.
Electric Cars
Advantages
  • Electric cars do not have an exhaust pipe so they cannot generate emissions, which reduces air pollution and benefits the environment and people's health.
  • Another positive factor is its recycling. The batteries that power electric cars, as well as hybrids, are made of lithium-ion, weigh less and have greater autonomy, and can be recycled.
  • It is increasingly common to see electric cars charging.
  • Driving an electric car is smooth, with an instant start and no 3alexd / Getty gears.
  • The experience of driving an electric also makes a difference. From the cabin we hardly perceive any mechanical sound, and if something enhances its qualities are its smooth driving, instant acceleration and the fact that it does not have gears.
  • The absence of traditional mechanical components (no clutch, no filters, no timing belts ...) allows a reduction in breakdowns, and, therefore, savings in the form of maintenance costs.
Electric Cars
In a nutshell
As you can see, there are currently more negative than positive points, so we believe that the section of electric cars will not become the future of the automotive industry. In one tomorrow this niche will become stronger, and we will probably see more electric vehicles rolling in the streets, but they will simply serve as an excellent option to support the conservation of the environment and reduce the costs generated by fuels. On the other hand, hybrids are currently excellent options for the market. They are more reliable, offer a high level of efficiency and also largely preserve the essence of internal combustion vehicles.
