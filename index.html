Artificial Intelligence

But, What is Artificial Intelligence?

It is strange to hear that big data collects information to be able to create something else with it, as if with the information such great things could be built, because perhaps if that information is unified with a greater force such as artificial intelligence, in fact, the results obtained can be indescribable. Artificial intelligence are intelligent systems that perform work similar to that of humans.

It's already among us!

It has been mentioned that artificial intelligence is a little prominent term, however it is present in the day to day, something that is usually a topic of conversation is the changing algorithms of social networks, and that is one of the closest examples of artificial intelligence. It is impressive how before seeing the study materials I lived very closely with this type of intelligence, however, I did not know that the actions of our day to day like searching the least bit in Google are part of it.

An interesting point to discuss is the displacement we feel when we see how artificial intelligence solves big problems, however humans are the ones who teach the algorithm, so in one way or another we are both essential. Although there are jobs that are more likely to be replaced by artificial intelligence, it is not 100% safe, but it is concerning for those who work in these areas.

AI has already surpass our expectations...

However, a point that caught our attention and that we share is that before being afraid of what may happen in the future, it is better to use artificial intelligence as an ally to learn from it and enjoy the benefits that He offers us. As we prepare to be better human beings and professionals.

And although we may not be motivated to abound in this vast world of data, I agree 100% with the final sentence mentioned by the speaker Fredi Vivas: “human behavior and human knowledge, empathy and creativity, the ability to solving unexpected situations will never stop being necessary ”and if that is achieved through data analysis, obviously it is a leap that we all must take at some point.
